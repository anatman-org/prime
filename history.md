# The Game and AI

As [LearningCorp] was founded in the game,
it was natural that one of the first human-user interfaces
to the emerging ai framework was naturally,
the Archives.

The Archives,
the official record of games,
consists of the sum of all games,
with every frame, every sequence,
commented on, analyzed, and categorized
over thousands of years.
This provides a wealth of information
to any system being trained.
