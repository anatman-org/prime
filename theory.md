# Introduction

The mathematics of the game are rather simple.

The beads and the actions of the players are designed
to produce random outcomes.
But the outcomes, while random, are weighted.
They are weighted quite precisely.
They are weighted using ratios of primes.

For example.

In standard play,
there are 

    3 "blue" beads
    22 "black" beads
    (total 25=5^2 "yin")

    (total 25 "yang")
    16 "white" beads (=4^2)
    9 "clear" beads (=3^2)
