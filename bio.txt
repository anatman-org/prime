
novum lusor
taking the seat of Merton

an aspiring science fiction author
who stumbles across the idea of the game
described in Herman Hesse's book [Glass Bead Game]

the game play tells a story

a story about council of reviewers or commentators
some mysterious body that has gathered

and each person tells their own story
and in telling, comments on a larger story
that is unfolding

the larger story is

the Transformation of Things
