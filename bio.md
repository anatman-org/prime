---
title: root
---

the last of a long line of ritual archivists
obsessed with records of a game consisting of glass beads

back to the beginning

born a hopeless romantic
to a pair of hopeless romantics

trying his whole life to figure our one question
and one question only

why does he feel this immense love?
how is he so drawn to Her in every way?
as if every piece of him,
every life experience proves Her love for him?

in ways
in coincidences
unquantifiable
