title: 庄周
---
i remember

[@2.35]
i am a butterfly
happy as can be

unaware of
Zhuang Zhou

awaking suddenly
astonished

suddenly
Zhuang Zhou

but
i do not know

if i am Zhuang Zhou
who dreamt i was a butterfly

or a butterfly
dreaming now that i am Zhuang Zhou

though bewildered
i know

there must be some difference
between Zhuang Zhou and butterfly

this bewilderment is
the Transformation of Things
