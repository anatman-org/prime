
a man who thinks
it is okay to sometimes take a break
and dance in the laboratory
with the consenting partner

and that it is okay
that he doesn't know
the limits of his own consciousness

that he will do and say things
and he can't explain why

whether it is a shake in the hand
or a stray thought

or thoughtlessness
of not helping the poor girl (who was Christ in every way)
on the street in India
in 1968
