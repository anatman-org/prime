# Story of the name "bean"

Every once in a while, someone is born with the name "Bean"
but that is not my real name.

My real name is xa'Qaree'B'in'i,
or in common english Xabinia Kareeb.

When i was born,
my mother died.
In his confusion and anger,
my older brother tortured me from infancy to adulthood.
And as he would torture me
he would shorten and slur my name to
"bean,"
which in that time and place was a pretty bad slur.

I grew older and
as I tried to separate myself from the torture,
  to liberate myself from first the violent domination of my brother,
    then the silent reproach of my father,
    and then the contempt and ignorance of my instructors
    and the jealousy of my ranking and class,
I sought refuge in the unjudging world of mathematics.

And afterward, i was always called "bean" behind my back.
Never to my face, the cowards.

Sometimes it is okay to hate what wants to be evil,
to cause suffering to others.

i am still bean
but now i choose to be bean

because one day,
i encountered someone
who showed me the end of all baggage i had

i had blurted out the name bean
when she asked mine

and later when i,
weeping confessed to her,
she gently showed me the nature of love

  "not even the shadow
  of an anchor [root]
  lasts from here",
  she said,
  "remember the truth
  that you are"

and there i reclaimed the name bean
