---
title: thornton Prime
---

a plain dude
who obsesses about the strange coincidences
that seem to draw him to a game of glass beads

and finds the more honest he is
with himself

about who he is
and how he fits in the world

the more the strange coincidences seem to focus sharply
as if sending a message from the forgotten future

  when all coincidences coincide
  at a singularity
  at the end of causality
