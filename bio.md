a man

  for a long time dreamed
  of spinning the cleverest, shiny web he could
  of every truth he could glean
  
but
who really dreams of being
free of cares,
like a butterfly

and there are boy butterflies
and the shame of wanting to be that

without any burdens

  BECAUSE
  it was feminine (and what would be wrong with that, but it isn't)
  it was weak (the ones we are told will inherit the earth?)

  all boyish dreams of trying to live up to one's father

but now recognizing those burdens i have left others
