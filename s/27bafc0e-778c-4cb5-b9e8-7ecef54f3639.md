the four key virtues of Heaven
extolled in the *Yijing*:

    * yuan - sprouting, or the origin, the sublime initiative
    * heng - growing, or the sacrifice, prosperous and smooth
    * li - blooming, the harvest (with a knife), favorable and beneficial
    * zhen - bearing fruit, or discernment (divination), steadfast and upright
