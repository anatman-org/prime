# y20 > y72 / y02 > y27

"conflict" means to appear before the council

it does not really mean "conflict"
as much as it means
"seeking justice"

Wen sequences conflict72*] with needing[27]
to demonstrate the difference between
pleading for what one needs and
pleading for what one is deserved

even in Wen's very political perspective,
these are not opposed, of course

humans have inherent rights as sentient beings
and while these things may not be as essential as

  (clean) air
  (clean) water
  (healthy) food
  (safe) place of habitation

we still have a right, deep in our being, to

  freedom and connection

that is

  we have the choice to roam the expanses of reality
    within the boundaries of our unique experiences
      and within those others share with us
  
as much as

  we have the choice to offer sincerely our unique experiences with others
    hoping that they share something mutually beneficial

this is not something that requires pleading
because it cannot be done in hidden places

but for the offering to be sincere
we must account for the states of experience of others

and we will make mistakes
  and our offering will offend
and we will apologize and grow if we are sincere
  in our offering

so, beyond the narrow Wen reading
this is that tension

we share great needs with one another
  these are not trivial
  and if someone pleads on hands and knees
  i know that they are being deprived these basic rights

    by me

  i should never deprive anyone in this way willingly
  and i must in every earnest way make sure my life is less burden on others in this way

to all others we owe respect for their experiences and
  are entitled
    a sincere opportunity to share ourselves in a sincere gift
