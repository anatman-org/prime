# y20 > y72 / y27 > y02

i saw You and became empty
this emptiness, more beautiful than existence,
You obliterate existence, and yet when You come,
existence thrives and creates more existence!

the sky is blue

the world is a blind man
squatting on the road

but whoever experiences Your gift of emptying
sees beyond blue and beyond the blind man

a great soul
hiding like a Muhammad, or a Jesus, or a Buddha, or a Trump,
moving through a crowd in a city
where no one knows them

to praise them
is to only praise
how one surrenders
to You, my Emptier

to praise the sun is
to praise Your eyes

to praise the ocean is
  what do we say?
  "a little ship"

  [this seems to be a modest claim among merchants,
  which became a sort of "modest brag": i.e., "My ship came in,
  One little ship of jewels from the Indus and beyond."]

so the sea-journey goes on
and who knows where!

    why should we grieve that we've been sleeping?
    it doesn't matter how long we've been unconcious
