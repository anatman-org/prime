---
title: Orbiting 
wen: 17
---

the old script depicts this as
a flag staked in the ground,
with the dance of warriors circling around

this is the leader and followers,
the earth and the heavens,
the sun and the planets,
the dark center and the galaxy

where one is the center,
the others circle

as wen says,

    the still places itself at the center of the moving
    the firm places itself as the foundation of the yielding
    circling with delight

    great prosperity, virtue and strength, without fault
    all things follow their orbits
    in the harmonious rhythm of the seasons

thunder within a lake, movement within the water,
a black hole draws all things into its orbit
