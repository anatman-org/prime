Horns caught in the hedge.
  My own anger and hardness has got me in a bind.
It is time now to disentangle from the hedge.

The image here is that of relief, freedom.
It is also the image of a bull's horns being cut off,
because he will hurt himself and others if he is left with them.

A horned instrument, a sacred talisman.
Undoing knots.

now is the time to recuperate in peac e.
