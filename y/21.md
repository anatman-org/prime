---
title: Sprouting
wen: 3
---

cloud above
thunder below

movement of things hidden
alters the shape of things above
as roots, deep within, begin to sprout

huang calls this "beginning"
and that is an appropriate definition

the old script depicts this as
a seed, with both a twisting root extending below
and branches and leaves emerging from the husk

wen says,

    the beginning of a tiny sprout
    is an auspicious moment

    prosperous, smooth, steady, upright
    do not act lightly

    with objective in mind
    establish relations with others
