understanding the Truth of oneself
requires 

  the Way made clear and concrete is not the Way

  great distinction does not use words
    words make distinctions, falling short
  great benevolence does not appear charitable
    benevolence is particular, personal, and not universal
  great uprightness is not humble
    the clarity of uprightness does not inspire trust
  great courage has no room for self-control
    spending time in recalcitrance, one will not succeed
