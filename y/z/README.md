The greatest mystery of all time
is how did someone
as intelligent as Hugo Farnsworth,
come to depend so much on John A. Zoidberg?

While this is partially explained
by Farnsworth's continuing fear that
he has hyper-malaria,
and he has tasked Zoidberg with killing him
before his symptoms manifest.

But this is just the tip of the Zoidberg,
as aluded to in the name of the defining episode,
and confirmed by the fact that the incompetent Zoidberg
is retained as doctor for at least two more seasons.

There is,
in fact,
a clear conspiracy keeping Zoidberg
in his position.
The exact nature of this is unknown.

This project is a collection of
Farnsworth Deadly Machines,
which are relevant to the uncovering of
the conspiracy.
