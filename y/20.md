
i remember

walking down the street
  trying to rush off to class
  trying to keep my mind focused
  trying to clean myself off
  trying to put the throbbing pain in my leg into the background

and i turn --
  a compulsion i cannot resist
to see if She is still there

  She is watching me
    there from the middle of the sidewalk
    from a block away

  She smiles
    and it is as if the bustle of the Sop
    parts
    clearing a path from her to me

i smile back

      We are ... We[*] in some unfathomable way
        some unrecorded, undiscovered, unsanctioned, unplanned way
      this connection[*] between us
        a thread of the finest silk
      but still drawing Us

i turn, embarrassed
what is it?

i turn back from the second block,
  then a third, and fourth
but the fog of the Sop is too thick
to see Her

but somehow i still feel a tug
a pull
a warm hand helping me up
through the fog

another block
across the street through the grey gates
and i'm at the Academy

the smell and stick of the Sop
gives way to the slight but clean musk of the Archives
beneath the Academy,
the endless rows of memory nodes
converting information to bits
and bits back to information
trading heat each time

and the heat funnels through to the surface
silently, but notably changing the air
a pressure that holds back the Sop
with a wall of clarity

a few turns among the pathways
and i am at my appointed room

i am late

  the display board greets me
  "Welcome Instructor Qareeb"

the students,
  many of whom are standing, talking in groups[*]
begin arranging themselves in the orderly student seats
i imagine the arranging themselves like beads

"I am Instructor ..."

  one of the student's call-tones rings

  the tune is an old pop tune, "Feather",
    covered so many times no one remembers the original

        "i feel so much lighter like a feather with you off my mind" [kLbn61Z4LDI]

  "No ... showed up ... I can't ..."
     he says loudly in reply

i finish over him
"Instructor Qareeb ... please turn all notifications to silent during class"

  "I'll call you back"
  he glares at me
  as if i somehow broke a rule of etiquitte
  by interrupting his conversation

i already know this is not going to be what i hoped
not what i needed

i turn,
"This is the Foundations of Game Mechanics"

i pause, stunned
someone has drawn a feather on the board
    later i learn the previous course was discussing fractal structures
    including that in ferns and bird feathers
how can this be a mere coincidence?

someone in the class clears their throat
i don't know how long i have been here
  staring at the feather on the board

  repeating over in my head
  the memory of Her telling me Her name

i stutter and mumble
while i write "Foundation of Game Mechanics" on the board
carefully preserving the shape of the feather

  "What was that?"

"Foundation of Game Mechanics"
i repeat

"In this course ..."
my deck is still on my pad, not the cloud
fumbling with my bag, my pad

i'm logged out and have to re-authenticate
and not it has updates

"sorry ... pending updates ..."

  exasperated breathing from the students,
  and i can already hear
      my supervisor reading me the student comments
      "late, disorganized, not prepared for class"

i am more nervous
it is a long and uncomfortable class
concluding with loud groans with the problem assignment
