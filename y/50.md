Wen says,

  when my desire to advance is not met with
    the confidence and interest of those in power,
  i maintain a calm and generous attitude

  it is a grievous situation,
  but i must maintain that adherence to principles
  is its own reward

  and when i am in a position of influence
  i must remain genteel and reserved
  and not dwell in shame or guilt of what might have been better
  all right behavior is rewarded in time

Wen was always obsessed with political climbing.
