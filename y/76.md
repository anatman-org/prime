Su calls this "infiltration by inferior men"
Dan said, "don't get involved with a strong and confident woman"

those are wrong interpretations

this is best translated as 

    "deeply intimate encounter with a woman"

old script depicts this as a fecund woman,
her breast is full, her belly is full, a baby issues forth, crown shining
a queen has given birth to a prince

before that, it was just "the chute"
a euphemism for a vaginal canal,
the most intimate, sensitive  part of a woman

    does euphemism sound very much like eu-fem-ism?
    what might that word mean?

Huang puts it in sequence,

    "after eliminating, surely there will be something to encounter

the waters have receded after reaching back as far as they can, and now they press forward

    thus, after eliminating, encountering follows"

mother says

    this is the single yin line, encroaching on the "clear" yang
    starting at the bottom and pushing upward, slowly, gently
    as is her way

    like a wave in the ocean

    now the wave rushes to shore
    quickly running up the sands
    like electricity, from fingers and toes, to the belly, the core
    reaching, climaxing


it is the feminine sea climbing onto the masculine shore
as she reaches her peak moment
