 the approaching end of one cycle / the teacher and student
                                      the elder and child

      the end of the cycle        / a difficult but fortunate birth

the time to learn
is rapidly reaching its conclusion

when the end is reached
it will be a difficult transition
like dying and being reborn

  strange how we say "reborn" in the passive voice?
  as if it is no longer me  

but ultimately
it is for the best
in ways we can't imagine
