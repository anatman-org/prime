why do i prefer
lower case pronouns and proper nouns
for myself?

because upper case is reserved
for Proper Things
and i am not Proper

and because upper case marks
Proper Nouns -- People, Places, Things
that are unique

and i'm not unique

i'm one of a line of Thorntons
that extends before me
and after me

and upper case is also reserved for
The Beginning
of something, like a sentance

and all i have said
and all i have thought
is words that i have borrowed from Others

and in my confusion
i have gotten Them all mixed up
in my head

and because upper case marks
things that can be either object or subject
and i really am not either

i am always in-between
sometimes subject and sometimes object
in my own life story

i mean,
You see me as subject and object, right?
but it shifts?

but for me, it doesn't shift
it is constant awareness
more of a continuum 

a blur
of shifting experiences
but throughout there is only experience

there is nothing of "I"
that isn't past or future --
fear or desire

and those are all me -- object --
they are not "I" -- subject

everything else
is this image
cast upon this canvas

the canvas doesn't have any perspective
except that what is projected upon it

that's why
