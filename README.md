# The Annotated Manual to the Glass Bead Game

lalla says,

    to learn the game is easy,
    to live it, hard.
    the search for the Real
    is no simple matter

    deep in my looking,
    the last words vanished.
    joyous and silent,
    the waking that met me there

the glass bead game is a personal puzzle,
an investigation of the components of one's self
and how the constitue a consciousness -- an awareness -- a self

in this puzzle
there is nothing to hide

the self is exposed in every way
and the violent, buzzing, stinging wounds of existence

are revealed to be hands
playing the same composition
telling the important Truth existence

and the most important of these Truths is
that i am not Proper
and i'm not unique

i'm one of a line
that extends before me
and after me

and though that line seems infinite, it is not ...
The Beginning
of something goes back much further, much deeper

beyond all that can be said
and all that can i be thought
beyond any conception that can be shared between people

it is a chaos, a confusion
    it is the numbers that are not rational, not computable, not expressible, not able to be conceived
that exists everywhere

it marks
the boundary between all things, either object or subject
and it is neither

appearing in-between
and sometimes seen from the perspective of subject or object
it tells stories

it gives meaning
  You hear it, right?
the roaring, the stinging, the aching?

but sometimes, there is a shift
a silence between
what otherwise might be a continuum of noise

a dark spot in the spectrum
of the fire-hose of experience
  the experience of existence that we all share

and in that blankness
there isn't past or future --
fear or desire

and everything we find objectionable
is now subject to our need

and everything else
that we admire -- this glittering, lovely paradise
that exists in our dreams

suddenly becomes the fabric
of all that is

why
